# duckpond logo palette

Derived from `caspar_duckpond.jpg` and matched to the brand blue in
`caspar.water/site/img/logo.svg`.

| Role          | Hex       | Notes                                  |
|---------------|-----------|----------------------------------------|
| Brand navy    | `#00325a` | Exact blue from the wordmark SVG; used for the duck and outlines |
| Pond water    | `#1f6699` | Mid blue, reads as deeper than the sky |
| Pond ripple   | `#4a8cbd` | Lighter water highlight                |
| Sky           | `#cfe7f6` | Light blue                             |
| Green surround| `#5a8f3c` | Reeds / shrubs                         |
| Green shadow  | `#3c6c28` | Darker foliage                         |

## Files

- `duckpond-icon-minimal.svg`  - monochrome `#00325a` duck on ripples
- `duckpond-scene-badge.svg`   - rounded badge: sky + pond + green surround + tiny duck
- `duckpond-duck-silhouette.svg` - duck silhouette only (app-icon friendly)
- `duckpond-two-tone.svg`      - duck + water in `#00325a` with lighter accent tint
